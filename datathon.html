Spring 2025 Datathon

The Datathon 2025 is a collaborative data-driven competition where participants analyze weather and emissions data to uncover meaningful insights. This event provides a hands-on learning experience, welcoming both technical and non-technical participants interested in data analytics, environmental science, and machine learning. Teams of up to four members will retrieve, visualize, and model real-world data to identify trends in emissions forecasting. No team? No problem! We will help you find the right group to collaborate, learn, and compete for the best data-driven solutions.
